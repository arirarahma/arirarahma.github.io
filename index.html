LOVELYZ (러블리즈)

On November 12, 2014, a flower bloomed. Babysoul, Jiae, Jisoo, Mijoo, Kei, Jin, Sujeong, and Yein, eight talented girls started their musical career in a family named Lovelyz. Debuted with "Candy Jelly Love" and followed by "Hi~" and "Ah-Choo", Lovelyz offer refreshing choice for listeners who prefer cutesy and sweet music. With more than 350k album copies sold, Lovelyz has set themselves as one of the household name in the kpop industry with strong vocal and captivating performance.
